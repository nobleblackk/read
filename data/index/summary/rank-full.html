Full Rank of matrix \(A\)

For a \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\) of rank \(r\) and \(m = n\).
\(\text{Rank}(A)=r=n\) it means that all of the column vectors are independent.
\(\bullet\)  # pivots \(=n\) \(\Rightarrow\) # free column vectors \(=0\).

Here we have no dependent column vectors here so,
\(\bullet\)  Null Space of \(A\) is just \(\vec{0}\).

So complete solution is just a single vector \(\vec{x}_p\) and it is in the column space of our matrix \(A\).
\(\bullet\)  # solutions \(=1\)
