Basis of fundamental spaces

Say we have a \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\), \(A\in\mathbb{R}^{m\times n}\) and rank of matrix is \(r\)

\(\bullet\)  Basis of Column space are those \(r\) independent columns of matrix \(A\)

\(\bullet\)  Basis of Row space are those \(r\) independent rows of matrix \(A\)

\(\bullet\)  Basis of Null space are those \(n-r\) special solutions, which we can get by solving \(A\vec{x}=\vec{0}\)

\(\bullet\)  Basis of Left Null space are those \(m-r\) special solutions, which we can get by solving \(A^T\vec{x}=\vec{0}\)
