Full Row Rank of matrix \(A\)

For a \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\) of rank \(r\) and \(m\lt n\).
\(\text{Rank}(A)=r=m\) now all of the column vectors are not independent.
▶  # pivots \(=m\) \(\Rightarrow\) # dependent column vectors \(=n-r\).

Here we have \(n-r\) dependent column vectors so,
▶  Null Space of \(A\) is in \(\mathbb{R}^{n-r}\).

So complete solution have \(\infty\) possible solutions.
▶  # solutions are \(\infty\)
