Dimensions of fundamental spaces

Say we have a \(m\times n\) matrix \(A\), \(A\in\mathbb{R}^{m\times n}\) and rank of matrix is \(r\)

\(\bullet\)  Column space lives in \(r\) dimensional vector space, \(C(A)\in\mathbb{R}^r\)

\(\bullet\)  Row space also lives in \(r\) dimensional vector space, \(C(A^T)\in\mathbb{R}^r\)

\(\bullet\)  Null space lives in \(n-r\) dimensional vector space, \(N(A)\in\mathbb{R}^{n-r}\)

\(\bullet\)  Left Null space lives in \(m-r\) dimensional vector space, \(N(A^T)\in\mathbb{R}^{m-r}\)
