Orthogonal Subspaces

Say we have two subspaces \(\mathcal{S}\) and \(\mathcal{U}\).
\(\mathcal{S}\) and \(\mathcal{U}\) are orthogonal if, every vectors in \(\mathcal{S}\) is orthogonal to every vectors in \(\mathcal{U}\).
Our \(4\) fundamental subspaces are related as:
\(\text{ Column Space }\) is orthogonal to \(\text{ Left Null Space } \)
\(\text{ Row Space }\) is orthogonal to \(\text{ Null Space } \)
